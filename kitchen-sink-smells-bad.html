Why Does My Kitchen Sink Smell Bad?

A bad smell coming from your kitchen sink is usually caused by food buildup, grease, or bacteria inside the drain pipes.

Common Causes of Kitchen Sink Odors

Food and Grease Buildup

Food scraps and grease can stick to the inside of pipes, creating bacteria that cause unpleasant smells.

Dry or Dirty P-Trap

If the sink is not used often, the P-trap can dry out, allowing sewer gases to escape into your kitchen.

Is a Smelly Kitchen Sink Dangerous?

In most cases, it is not dangerous, but strong sewer smells may indicate plumbing issues that need attention.

How to Get Rid of Kitchen Sink Smells

  • Flush the drain with hot water
  • Use baking soda and vinegar
  • Clean the P-trap if possible
  • Avoid pouring grease down the sink

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my sink smell worse after washing dishes?

Hot water can release trapped odors from grease and food buildup.

Can sink odors come from the dishwasher?

Yes, food residue in the dishwasher drain can cause smells.
