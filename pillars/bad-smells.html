Why Do Bad Smells Appear in Homes?

Unpleasant odors inside homes usually indicate hidden issues such as moisture buildup, plumbing problems, mold growth, or poor ventilation.

Common Types of Household Odors

  • Musty smells
  • Sewer and drain odors
  • Kitchen and food smells
  • Nighttime room odors

Prevention Tips

Regular ventilation, moisture control, and proper drain maintenance can prevent most odor-related problems before they become serious.
